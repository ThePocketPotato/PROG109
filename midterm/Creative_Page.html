Bouldering
Bouldering is a form of rock climbing that does not utilize ropes. To replace the loss of a key safety mechanic people who boulder either boulder inside or if they are outside they use a soft pad called a "crash pad" to break their fall. I have been bouldering for around 8 months and I have enjoyed every minute of it. Bouldering is such a good sport because it doesn't matter how good you are, there is a climb for you.
